Due to the custom, print-on-demand nature of our products, we can only accept returns in these cases:

‚ú® Product Care

While our mugs can technically go in the dishwasher, we strongly recommend hand washing to preserve the design quality and extend the life of your mug.

If you do use a dishwasher, place the mug on the top rack and use a gentle cycle with mild detergent. Avoid high heat settings.

Yes! Our mugs are microwave-safe. You can safely reheat your coffee, tea, or hot chocolate. However, avoid extremely long heating times and always use caution when removing hot mugs from the microwave.

Best practices for long-lasting mugs:

  • Hand wash with mild soap and warm water
  • Use a soft sponge (avoid abrasive scrubbers)
  • Dry with a soft cloth
  • Avoid soaking for extended periods
  • Store in a safe place where it won't get knocked over
  • Don't use harsh chemicals or bleach-based cleaners

With proper care, your sarcastic masterpiece will last for years!

Our mugs are printed using high-quality, professional-grade printing technology. When properly cared for (hand washing, avoiding abrasive cleaners), the designs are very durable and should not fade or peel. If you experience any issues with design quality, contact us - this would be covered under our quality guarantee!
